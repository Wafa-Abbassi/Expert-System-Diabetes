Private Online GP Consultations Service

Europe’s largest remote digital GP consultation platform. The first to provide video consultation, private prescription and fit note services, reaching millions of users.

Read more

Now GP provides users with a private online GP consultation service to support individuals who are unable to get an appointment with their GP or who choose to access private healthcare.

  • GP Consultations by video at anytime, from anywhere
  • Digital medication and treatment adherence
  • Fast answers by our clinicians to your health questions
  • 24/7 access
  • Artificial Intelligence symptom checking
  • Safe and secure encryption
  • Choice of GP based on gender, language and specialist clinical interests
  • Convenience of healthcare when you need it
  • Private Prescriptions and Test Kits

NHS Online Pharmacy & Digital GP Consultation
NHS Online Pharmacy & Digital GP Consultation

Providing NHS repeat prescriptions. Working in partnership with the NHS to provide 15 million people with access to remote GP consultations and free medication delivery.

Read more

Now Patient is a service offered to the 15 million chronic care patients in the UK which account for £7 billion per annum of the NHS medicines budget.

We are uniquely placed, to deliver the only National Health Service based product that will be able to help the NHS meet a number of the key targets for the NHS Five Year Forward View targeted for 2020.

  • NHS Repeat Prescriptions delivered to your door
  • Already works with your NHS GP
  • GP Consultations by video at anytime, from anywhere
  • Digital medication and treatment adherence
  • Fast answers by our clinicians to your health questions
  • 24/7 access
  • Artificial Intelligence symptom checking
  • Safe and secure encryption
